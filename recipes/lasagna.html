Lasagna

Description

Veg lasagna is a delicious vegetarian twist on the classic Italian dish. Layers of lasagna noodles alternate with savory fillings, creating a satisfying and comforting meal. Instead of meat, a veggie lasagna is packed with roasted or sauteed vegetables like zucchini, mushrooms, spinach, and bell peppers. These are bathed in a rich tomato sauce and often layered with creamy ricotta cheese or a similar cheesy mixture.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: zucchini, mushrooms, spinach, bell peppers (or your favorites)
  • Lasagna noodles (no-boil or regular work)
  • Tomato sauce (jarred or homemade)
  • Ricotta cheese or cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil and seasonings

Steps

  1. Saut√© or roast your chosen vegetables.
  2. If using regular noodles, cook them according to package directions.
  3. Mix ricotta with herbs and seasonings (optional).
  4. Layer a baking dish with sauce, noodles, ricotta, vegetables, and cheese.
  5. Repeat layers, ending with cheese.
  6. Bake until bubbly and golden brown.
