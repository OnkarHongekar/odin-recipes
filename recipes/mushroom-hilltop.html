Mushroom Hilltop

Description

This is a common recipe that could be associated with "Hilltop" due to featuring mushrooms and potentially using wild mushrooms grown on a hilltop.

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms (baby bella, cremini, or a mix)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Celery (optional)
  • Butter or oil
  • Flour
  • Vegetable broth
  • Potatoes (diced)
  • Herbs (thyme, bay leaf)
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Saut√© chopped onion, celery (if using), and garlic in butter or oil until softened.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in flour and cook for a minute to create a roux.
  4. Gradually whisk in vegetable broth, scraping up any browned bits.
  5. Add diced potatoes, herbs, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then simmer until potatoes are tender.
  6. (Optional) Stir in milk or cream for a richer chowder.
  7. Taste and adjust seasonings as needed.
